EMPLOYEE WELL-BEING AND COVID-19
In these uncertain times, you have access to benefits meant to keep you well,
physically and emotionally.
COVID-19 Testing – Evening Post Industries is covering the cost of COVID-19 testing for those
enrolled in a medical plan. Note this only applies to office visits, urgent care facilities and clinics, etc.
COVID-19 Treatment - From Jan 31 - May 31, 2020, Evening Post Industries has agreed to waive BCBSSC
member cost-sharing, including deductible, copayments and coinsurance related to treatment for
COVID-19. The waiver applies to costs associated with COVID-19 treatment at in-network facilities and
treatment for out-of-network emergencies.
